World Event:

To trigger the event, simply click the left mouse button.
This will cause a 5x5 meadow of flowers to appear around the mouse pointer's position.
A wyvern (the new entity) will appear in the center of this meadow where the mouse pointer is.
Any Ore Blobs that exist in the world will be transformed into wolves (Creature).
The wolves will chase after any MinerNotFull entities and trigger a quake animation.
The wyvern will chase after any wolves and trigger a mini fire explosion animation (Flare).
